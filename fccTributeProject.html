Who Is Esperanza Spalding?

  • American bassist, singer, songwriter, and composer
  • Born October 18, 1984 Portland, Oregon
  • five Grammy Awards, a Boston Music Award, a Soul Train Music Award
  • Attended Portland State University and Berklee College of Music on scholarship
  • first album, Junjo, in 2006 on the Spanish label Ayva Musica
  • Her third studio album, Chamber Music Society (2010), was a commercial success, charting at number 34 on the Billboard 200, and resulting in Spalding winning her first Grammy Award for Best New Artist

"You don't have to be fearless to do anything; you can be scared out of your mind. I fear that I won't get better and that I won't have time to practice. To be called a 'jazz musician' - it's a big responsibility."

- Esperanza Spalding
